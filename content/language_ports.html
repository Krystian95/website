JOLIE is all about communicating with services. You can invoke a service or be the service that gets invoked. Programs get more complex by composing message sendings and/or receivings; as such, the first fundamental notion for programming with JOLIE is to understand how to define services and how to communicate.

Ports

> Go on top

Ports are necessary both for receiving and sending messages. In Jolie every communication is fulfilled by means of ports. Three are two kind of ports:

  • Input ports: they are used for receiving messages from other services
  • Output ports: they are used for sending messages to other services
