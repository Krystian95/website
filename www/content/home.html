Jolie is the first fully-fledged service-oriented programming language. It brings elegance, simplicity, and pragmatism to the software development of applications characterised by modularity, multi-core support, concurrency, distribution, and integration. Be you an enterprise software project leader, a freelance programmer, a casual hacker, or a computer science researcher, you will find in Jolie a notion of simplicity in programming service-oriented software that you never saw before.
