Jolie provides a unique linguistic domain for addressing different issues of distributed programming in a service oriented style. Thanks to Jolie you can build distributed systems starting from the development of simple services and orchestrators to the composition of complex architectures.

  • Simple services: you can build simple services starting from scratch. You can connect them to databases such as SQLServer, PostgreSQL, MySQL and so on and provide service operations for extracting data from them. Deploying a Jolie service it is very simple, just type jolie followed by the service file.
  • Orchestrator: you can build new services by orchestrating the existing ones by developing more complex services called orchestrators. Differently from standard SOA technologies, in Jolie there are no differences between services and orchestrators thus programmers can easily approach them in the same way.
  • Web Applications: in Jolie web applications are considered as service applications where the web GUI interacts by means of AJAX calls directly with the service operations
  • System composition: you can integrate and compose exixting SOAs by using the powerful composition mechanisms of Jolie such as Aggregation, Redirection and Courier sessions
