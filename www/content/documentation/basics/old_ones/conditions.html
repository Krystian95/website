Conditions

Conditions are used in control flow statements in order to check a boolean expression. As such, conditions always evaluate either as true or false. Conditions can simply be an expression or a comparison. You can make comparisons by using the following operators:

Operators

is equal to==
is not equal to!=
is minor than<
is major than>
is minor or equal than<=
is major or equal than>=
negation!
Examples of valid conditions are:
  • x == "Hi!"
  • x < 2
  • 3 > x
  • 25 <= x
  • x >= 10
