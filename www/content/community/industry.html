italianaSoftware

italianaSoftware s.r.l. is a research spin-off that centers its business around the development of advanced service-oriented solutions with the JOLIE language and consulting activities for any kind of customer requirement about SOA design and implementation. At the present italianaSoftware operates within the innovation center of Imola: Innovami (www.innovami.it).

Link: www.italianasoftware.com
EOS

EOS srl develops web applications for business management, focusing in particular on the tasks of data integration and the handling of decisional processes. EOS srl develops products exploiting the JOLIE technology, actively contributing in the design and test processes of the integration of JOLIE with legacy/modern data systems and with modern web technologies.

Link: www.eos-software.it
